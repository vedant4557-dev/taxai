🔒 Privacy Policy

TaxSmart India · Last updated: February 2026

← Back to TaxSmart

✅ Short version: We never store your documents or personal financial data. Everything is processed in memory and deleted immediately. We don't sell data. We don't have ads.

📄 What data do we collect?

When you upload documents for auto-extraction:

  • Your PDF files (Form 16, Form 26AS, AIS) are sent to our secure server for processing.
  • Extracted data (salary, TDS, deductions) is returned to your browser and never saved on our end.
  • No account required — we don't collect your name, email, or phone number.

When you use the manual calculator (without uploading documents), no data ever leaves your browser. All calculations happen locally.

🗑️ Data deletion — immediate and automatic

Your uploaded PDFs are held in server memory only during processing (typically 5–15 seconds). They are:

  • Never written to disk or any database
  • Never logged or saved in any form
  • Automatically deleted from memory as soon as extraction completes
  • Never shared with any third party except Anthropic's API for extraction

🤖 Anthropic Claude API

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to read your PDF documents and extract tax data. When you upload a document:

  • Your PDF is sent to Anthropic's API over an encrypted HTTPS connection
  • Anthropic processes it and returns structured data (JSON)
  • Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to this processing
  • We use Anthropic's API-tier, which does not use your data to train their models

🍪 Cookies and tracking

We use no tracking cookies, no analytics, no ads. TaxSmart is a clean tool built for you, not to monetize your attention.

Your browser may store form values temporarily in session memory so you don't lose your work if you accidentally navigate away — this data never leaves your device.

🔐 Security

  • HTTPS All data in transit is encrypted via TLS/HTTPS
  • No storage Zero persistent storage of financial documents
  • Rate limited Our API limits requests to prevent abuse
  • No accounts No passwords, no personal accounts to breach

⚖️ Legal disclaimer

TaxSmart is a planning and estimation tool only. It is not a substitute for professional CA advice. Tax calculations are based on publicly available rules for FY 2025–26 and may not account for all individual circumstances.

Always verify your final tax liability with a qualified Chartered Accountant before filing your ITR.
