📄 What data do we collect?

When you upload documents for AI extraction:

  • Your PDF files (Form 16, Form 26AS, AIS) are transmitted over HTTPS to our secure backend server for processing.
  • Extracted data (salary figures, TDS amounts, deductions) is returned to your browser only. It is never saved on our servers.
  • No account required — we do not collect your name, email, phone number, or Aadhaar during document processing.
  • Optional email capture — if you choose to save your report or set a reminder, your email is stored locally in your browser only (localStorage). We do not transmit it to our servers unless you explicitly opt in to reminders.

When you use the manual calculator (without uploading documents), no data ever leaves your browser.

🗑️ Document deletion — immediate and automatic

Your uploaded PDFs are held in server RAM only during AI processing (typically 10–45 seconds). They are:

  • Never written to disk or any database at any stage
  • Never logged or retained in any persistent form
  • Automatically garbage-collected by Node.js immediately after the API response is sent
  • Never shared with any party except Google Gemini AI for extraction processing
  • Not accessible to any TaxSmart team member during or after processing

🚨 Data Breach Notification Policy

In the unlikely event of a security breach involving personal data, TaxSmart will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach (where contact information is available)
  • Report to the Data Protection Board of India as required under Section 8(6) of the DPDP Act 2023
  • Publish a breach notice on this page within 72 hours describing: nature of breach, data affected, steps taken, and remediation
  • Cooperate fully with the Data Protection Board investigation

Given our zero-storage architecture (no documents written to disk, no database of financial data), the practical risk of a meaningful data breach is extremely low. There is no database to breach.

🤖 Google Gemini AI Processing

We use Google Gemini AI to read your PDF documents and extract tax data. When you upload a document:

  • Your PDF is transmitted to Google's API over encrypted HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
  • Google processes it and returns structured data (JSON numbers only)
  • Google's Privacy Policy and API Terms apply to this processing
  • We use the API-tier which does not use your data to train Google's AI models
  • Google does not retain your document after processing under API terms

⚖️ Legal Basis for Processing (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Consent — You voluntarily upload your documents to receive tax extraction services. By clicking "Extract & Auto-fill", you provide explicit consent for processing.
  • Legitimate purpose — Processing is necessary to provide the core service (tax calculation and error detection) you have requested.
  • Data minimisation — We extract only the tax-relevant numerical values required for calculation. We do not extract or store personal identifiers beyond PAN (which is auto-filled for your convenience).

You may withdraw consent at any time by simply not uploading documents. The manual calculator works entirely without data processing.

🍪 Cookies and tracking

We use no tracking cookies, no analytics platforms, no advertising networks. TaxSmart is a clean tool built for you, not to monetise your attention.

Your browser may store form values in localStorage so you don't lose your work — this data never leaves your device and you can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

🔐 Security measures

  • TLS 1.3 All data in transit encrypted via HTTPS
  • Memory-only Zero persistent storage of financial documents
  • Rate limited 10 extractions per IP per hour to prevent abuse
  • Schema validated All AI outputs validated against strict schema before use
  • No accounts No passwords, no personal accounts to breach
  • File validation Only valid PDFs accepted; file type and MIME verified
  • Size limits Maximum 10MB per file to prevent DoS attacks
